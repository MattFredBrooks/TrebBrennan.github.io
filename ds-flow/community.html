How it works

We want to help designers and developers across government transform their agency’s products and services. To do this we need to grow an active and engaged community of users like you to help develop up a set of quality tools and designs for use by us all.


Free

The Design System is licensed under the MIT licence which means your team can build whatever you need it with.


Maintained

The DTA has a dedicated team of designers and developers working constantly on improving the Design System so that it's up to date.
